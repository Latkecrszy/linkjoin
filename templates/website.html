Being on time has never been so easy.

LinkJoin helps you join meetings automatically at exactly the right moment, even when you forget.

What is LinkJoin?

+
-
LinkJoin is a website that automatically opens your meetings for you. It's as simple as making an account and scheduling your links, and you'll never be late again.

How do I get started?

+
-
Just head over to the signup page and create an account. From there, you'll be redirected to the links page to start scheduling your meetings!

Why are my links not opening?

+
-
There are a few reasons that your links may not be opening. One of the first things that you should check for is that you have pop-ups for linkjoin.xyz enabled. See the other questions for instructions on how to do this. If you have popups enabled, next take a look at your link on the links page. You should see a day (or multiple) on it. This will look like the common abbreviation of the day (Mon, Tue, etc.) If you don't see any days there, click the edit button to select some days for your link to open on. If all else fails, feel free to send an email to me at setharaphael7@gmail.com, and I'd be happy to help figure out what's going on!
