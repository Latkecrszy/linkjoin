LinkJoin
Being on time has never been so easy.
LinkJoin helps you join meetings automatically at exactly the right moment, even when you're not at the computer.
What is LinkJoin?
LinkJoin is a site that automatically opens your meetings for you. Zoom, Google Meets, you name it! It's as simple as making an account and creating a link, and you'll never be late again.
How do I get started?
Just head over to the signup page and create an account. From there, you'll be redirected to the links page to start scheduling your meetings!
How does it work?
Here at LinkJoin, we provide only the best of service. That's why we use expertly trained snakes (pythons, to be specific) to click the buttons in our HQ that open your meetings right on time! Don't worry, they're very accurate.
