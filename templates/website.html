What is LinkJoin?
LinkJoin is a site that can schedule your meetings for you. Now that everything is virtual, you have likely come across a common problem. It's hard to remember things! That's the beauty of LinkJoin. Just head to this page and set a day and time for your meeting link to open. That's it! From there, LinkJoin will automatically start meetings for you, and you can get to everything on time.
